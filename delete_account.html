Delete Your COCO LEARN Account

COCO LEARN is developed by Ubaidur Rahman. This page explains how you can request deletion of your COCO LEARN account and the data associated with it.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to ubaidwasiq@gmail.com from the email address associated with your COCO LEARN account.
  2. Use the subject line: "COCO LEARN Account Deletion Request".
  3. Include the email address or username used to sign in to the app (this helps us locate your account).
  4. We will verify your request and delete your account and associated data within 30 days.
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.
Request Deletion via Email

What Data Is Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data is permanently deleted from our systems:

  • Account credentials (email address, hashed password, or linked Google Sign-In identifier)
  • Profile information (child's display name or nickname, if provided)
  • Learning progress data (phonics progress, speech game scores, activity history)
  • Any app preferences or settings tied to your account

What Data May Be Retained

COCO LEARN does not retain any data beyond what is described above. Once your account deletion request is processed, all account information, profile data, and learning progress stored in our database are permanently removed. We do not keep separate logs, analytics records, or backups containing your personal data.

The only exception is where retention is required to comply with a legal obligation, in which case data would be kept only as long as required by law.

Partial Deletion (In-App)

If you would like to delete specific progress data without deleting your entire account, you can do so directly within the app via Settings → Account → Clear Progress Data (if available in your app version).
